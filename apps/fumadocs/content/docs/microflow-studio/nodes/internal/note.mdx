---
title: Note
description: Add text notes and labels to your flow for documentation and organization.
---

The Note node lets you place a block of text anywhere on the canvas. It has no effect on how your flow runs — it is purely for documentation.

**When to use it:**
- Label a complex section so it is easier to understand at a glance
- Document why you wired something a specific way
- Leave a reminder about something to come back to
- Explain how a flow works so others — or your future self — can follow it

Notes are especially useful in flows you plan to share, or any project you might revisit later and wonder "what was I thinking here?"
