---
title: Monitor
description: Display values as they pass through your flow for debugging and understanding.
---

The Monitor node shows you what is happening inside your flow in real-time. Place it anywhere in your flow, and it displays the values passing through that point — as a number, a graph over time, or an on/off state.

It does not change your flow in any way. It is purely for observation.

**When to use it:**
- Your flow is not behaving as expected and you want to see what values your sensors are actually producing
- You want to verify that a [Map](/docs/microflow-studio/nodes/shape/map) or [Calculate](/docs/microflow-studio/nodes/shape/calculate) node is producing the right output
- You are building something new and want to watch how values change as they move through nodes

**Tip:** Place a Monitor node just *before* the node that is not working as expected. If the values arriving look wrong, the problem is upstream of that point. If they look correct, the problem is in that node's settings.
