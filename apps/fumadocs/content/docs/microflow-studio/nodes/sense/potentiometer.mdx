---
title: Potentiometer
description: Use a rotary or sliding potentiometer to control values in your flow.
---

A potentiometer is a dial or slider that you can turn or slide to set a value anywhere between its minimum and maximum. Think of it like a volume knob — turning it one way gives you a low value, turning it the other way gives you a high value.

A potentiometer can be used to control the brightness of an LED, the speed of a motor, the pitch of a buzzer, or any other value in your flow.

## 2 channel sliding potentiometer
Some sliding potentiometers have two channels. This means that the potentiometer has two separate resistive tracks. This can be useful when you want to control two separate devices with one potentiometer.
