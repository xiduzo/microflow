---
title: Compare
description: Check if values meet a condition and route your flow based on true or false results.
---

The Compare node checks whether a value meets a condition — greater than, less than, equal to, and so on. Based on whether the check passes or fails, it routes a signal down one of two paths.

**Example:** If a temperature sensor reads above 80°C, send a signal to turn on a fan. Otherwise, send a signal to keep the fan off.

The Compare node fires on every incoming value. When you want to detect a *crossing point* (like "fire once when the value rises above 80") use the [Trigger](/docs/microflow-studio/nodes/generate/trigger) node instead.

## Outputs

- **True** — fires when the condition is met
- **False** — fires when the condition is not met

Both outputs fire on every incoming value, so your flow reacts continuously as readings arrive.

## Settings

Open the settings panel to configure:

- **Condition** — the comparison to apply: greater than (>), less than (<), equal to (=), greater than or equal (≥), less than or equal (≤), not equal (≠)
- **Value** — the number to compare against
