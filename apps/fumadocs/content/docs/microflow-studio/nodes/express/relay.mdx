---
title: Relay
description: Control high voltage devices using a relay switch from a low-power microcontroller signal.
---

A relay is a remotely controlled switch. Your Arduino sends a small signal to the relay, and the relay uses that to switch a completely separate, higher-power circuit on or off. This lets you control things like lamps, fans, or motors that require more power than an Arduino can provide directly.

**Example:** Use a button press in Microflow to turn a desk lamp on or off.

<Callout type="warn" title="DANGER: LETHAL VOLTAGE">
Working with high voltage can be `DEADLY`. Even a momentary lapse in caution can result in severe injury or `DEATH`.

Never attempt to handle high voltage circuits without proper knowledge and always work under the supervision of a qualified expert. Your life is on the line.
</Callout>
