---
title: Interval
description: Automatically send a signal at regular time intervals, like a timer or clock.
---

An Interval node fires a signal on a repeating schedule — every second, every 500 milliseconds, every 5 minutes, whatever you set. Unlike a sensor that only fires when something changes, an Interval fires on its own whether or not anything else is happening.

**Examples:**
- Blink an LED on and off once per second
- Read a sensor value every 5 minutes and log it
- Nudge a servo position every 100 milliseconds to animate it smoothly
- Trigger a check at regular intervals in an automated flow

## Settings

Open the settings panel to configure:

- **Interval** — how often the signal fires, in milliseconds. 1000 ms = 1 second. 500 ms = twice per second. 60000 ms = once per minute
